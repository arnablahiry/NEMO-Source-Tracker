Bases: tkinter.Toplevel

Browse per-channel 2D wavelet coefficient maps with embedded parameters.

Number of scales is chosen via radio buttons (2…max), where max depends on the image dimensions. The figure is always laid out as 2 rows × ceil(n/2) cols. Changing n_scales re-runs the starlet transform and redraws in real time.
